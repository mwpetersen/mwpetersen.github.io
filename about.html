About

My name is Mikkel Wittenburg Petersen. I work at the University of Copenhagen, where I analyze data about the university's students and educations in order to inform the decisions of the management of the university.

I'm a sociologist by traning from Roskilde University and The City University of New York. In my studies I focused on education and quantitative methods.

In my spare time I hang out with my family, and when I have a free moment I do small data analysis projects using R and Python.
