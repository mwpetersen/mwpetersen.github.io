About

My name is Mikkel Wittenburg Petersen. I work as a Data Scientist at Clever, where I analyze data about the attitudes and behaviors of Clever's customers in order to inform decisions about product and concept development.

I'm a sociologist by training from Roskilde University and The City University of New York. In my studies I focused on education and quantitative methods.

In my spare time I hang out with my family, and when I have a free moment I like to try to learn something new by doing small data science projects using Python and R. You can take a look at those projects here.
